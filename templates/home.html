General Tips:

  • Figures are drawn to scale (sometimes you can just eyeball the answer).
  • Sketch out shapes.
  • Questions with figures - DON'T READ - skip to the end.
  • Italicized units means you will need to convert.
  • Use Process of Elimination (POE) to cross out nonsensical answers.
  • If you can't figure it out, try working backwards (guess and check).
